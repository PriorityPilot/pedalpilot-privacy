Frequently Asked Questions

Why does PedalPilot need my location?

PedalPilot uses your GPS location to display your position on the map and provide turn-by-turn cycling navigation. Without location access, the map will still display but navigation features will not work.

Does the app work offline?

PedalPilot requires an internet connection to search for destinations, calculate routes, and load map tiles. Once a route is displayed, basic navigation can continue briefly if connectivity drops, but a stable connection is recommended.

How do I change between metric and imperial units?

Open the Settings tab (gear icon) and tap the units option to switch between kilometres and miles.

How do I switch the language?

PedalPilot supports English and German. Go to the Settings tab and select your preferred language.

Does PedalPilot use background location?

Only during active navigation. When you start a route, PedalPilot continues tracking your position if you lock the screen or switch apps so you still receive turn-by-turn voice instructions. Background location stops immediately when navigation ends.

Is my data shared or sold?

No. PedalPilot stores all data locally on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no ads. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I report a bug?

Send us an email at berghausstudio@gmail.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS/Android version. Screenshots are very helpful.

System Requirements

  • iOS: iPhone running iOS 16.0 or later
  • Android: Android 8.0 (API 26) or later
  • Internet connection required for route calculation and map tiles
  • GPS/Location Services must be enabled
